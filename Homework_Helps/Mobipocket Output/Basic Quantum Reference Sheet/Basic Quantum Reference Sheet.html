1.       Spherical coordinates are represented in the way standard for chemists (which is different from the standard used by mathematicians).  i.e. r = radius from origin,  angle with the positive z axis,  angle between the positive x axis and the projection onto the x/y plane.

2.       This reference sheet was written shortly after I finished an introductory quantum chemistry course in my senior year. It was originally meant to help me remember that course in years to come, and while the quality standards have increased the scope has remained the same. It will not likely be helpful at all to one who does not already understand how to use these equations.
